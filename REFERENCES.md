## General Resources

These resources span multiple chapters and provide comprehensive coverage:

- **Qiskit Textbook** — IBM's free interactive quantum computing textbook: [IBM Quantum Learning](https://learning.quantum.ibm.com/)
- **Preskill's Caltech Lecture Notes (Ph219)** — Graduate-level quantum information course: [Caltech Ph219](http://theory.caltech.edu/~preskill/ph219/ph219_2023-24/)
- **Scott Aaronson, "Quantum Computing Since Democritus"** — Lecture notes bridging CS theory and quantum mechanics: [Lecture Notes](https://www.scottaaronson.com/democritus/)
